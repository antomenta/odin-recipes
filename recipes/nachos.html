Pulled pork nachos

Nacho regular appetizer

The perfect snack to share with your friends!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of tortilla chips
  • 500g shredded cheese for melting
  • 400g pre-seasoned pulled pork
  • 1 medium tomato
  • 0.25 lettuce
  • 0.5 bell pepper
  • 0.5 red onion
  • 0.3 cup of sliced olives
  • 1-2 jalapeño or banana peppers
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Cook the pulled pork according to the instructions on its packaging.
  2. While the pulled pork is cooking, preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  3. Chop your toppings: the tomato, the lettuce, the bell pepper, the onion and the jalapeño or banana peppers.
  4. Line an oven baking sheet with parchment paper and spread the tortilla chips over it.
  5. Pull apart the pulled pork.
  6. Layer half the cheese over the tortilla chips, then the pulled pork, and top with the rest of the cheese. This distribution will ensure cheesiness all over.
  7. Bake the loaded tortilla chips for five minutes.
  8. Wait five minutes for the nachos to cool down, and then cover with all the toppings you prepared on step 3, plus the sliced olives.
  9. Serve with sour cream, salsa and maybe some of that guac you made earlier.
