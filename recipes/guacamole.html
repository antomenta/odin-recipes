Guacamole

The dip that keeps on giving

Pair with our nachos, or use your favourite tortilla chips to spoon straight out of the bowl!

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 roma tomato
  • 0.5 red onion
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 tbsp of milk (you can substitute with greek yogurt)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Split the avocados in half and empty them into a bowl. Smash to desired chunkiness.
  2. Finely dice the tomato and the onion. Add to the bowl and mix.
  3. Juice the lime and add to bowl.
  4. Add the olive oil and the milk or greek yogurt to the bowl.
  5. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder and onion powder to taste and mix.
  6. Optional: finely chop cilantro to taste and mix it with the guacamole. You can also use cilantro as a garnish, to top it off.
  7. Serve with chips or as a side for our next recipe, nachos!
